#### **- Resolve a expressão booleana abaixo:**

#### 

#### 

#### **R = (V AND F) OR NOT(V AND F)**

#### 

#### **R= (F) OR NOT(F)**

#### **R = F OR V**

#### **R= V**

#### 

#### 

#### 

#### **- Resolva a expressão booleana abaixo:**

#### 

#### **R (V OR F)**

#### **R = F OR V**

#### **R= V AND NOT(F)**

#### **R= V AND V**

#### **R = V**

#### 

#### 

#### 

#### **R = NOT(V AND F) AND NOT(V OR F)**

#### **R = V AND F**

#### **R = NOT(F) AND NOT(V)**

#### **R = V AND F**

#### **R = F**

#### 

#### 

#### 

#### **R ((V AND F) AND (V OR F)) OR (V AND F)**

#### **R= ((F) AND (V)) OR (F)**

#### **R = (F AND V) OR F**

#### **R = (F) OR F**

#### **R = F OR F**

#### **R = F**

#### 

#### 

#### **R(V OR F) OR (NOT(V AND F) OR NOT(V OR F))**

#### 

#### **R= V OR (NOT (F) OR NOT(V)**

#### **R= V OR (V OR F)**

#### **R = V OR V**

#### **R = V**

#### 

#### **R = NOT(V OR F) AND (NOT(V OR F) OR (V AND F))**

#### 

#### **R = NOT (V) AND (NOT(V) OR (F)**
